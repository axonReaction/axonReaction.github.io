This is The Ironed Curtain Ian Bowman Jan 01 84

One day I woke up and decided it was time to contribute.

Ladies an gentleman, introducing THE IRONED CURTAIN. THE IRONED CURTAIN is the worlds first 7-bit website, a throwback to a simpler time when enemies become friends using 128 characters(TM).

(Note to the technically interested among you: THE IRONED CURTAIN is also Pelican blog theme written by yours truly, open sourced and available for download from github.com: url)

a bunch of text

sweet retro ascii logos

the unique brand of quality content that you expect when the name Ian Bowman is written next to it

Here's what you will not see:

a bunch of distracting motherfucking ads

bullshit graphics
